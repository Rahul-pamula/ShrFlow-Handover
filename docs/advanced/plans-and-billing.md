ShrFlow offers several tiers designed to scale with your operational complexity. All plans are billed monthly and include access to the core delivery engine.

## Comparison Table

| Feature | Free | Starter | Pro | Enterprise |
| :--- | :---: | :---: | :---: | :---: |
| **Monthly Emails** | 1,000 | 25,000 | 150,000 | 1,000,000+ |
| **Contacts** | 500 | 5,000 | 50,000 | 500,000+ |
| **Team Users** | 1 | 3 | Unlimited | Unlimited |
| **Domains** | 1 | 3 | Unlimited | Unlimited |
| **Price (Monthly)** | ₹0 | ₹799 | ₹2,499 | ₹9,999 |

## Plan Details

### Free
Ideal for testing the platform or running very small personal newsletters.
*   Basic analytics
*   Standard templates
*   Single user access

### Starter
Perfect for small product teams launching their first reliable sending setup.
*   Basic automation
*   Audience segmentation
*   Email support

### Pro (Recommended)
Designed for B2B teams running high-volume campaigns and requiring API access.
*   **API Access**: Integrate ShrFlow into your own applications.
*   **Advanced Automation**: Trigger emails based on complex events.
*   **Priority Support**: Faster response times for critical issues.

### Enterprise
For organizations needing dedicated infrastructure and tight governance.
*   **Dedicated IP**: Protect your sender reputation with isolated IPs.
*   **SSO/SAML**: Secure team access via your enterprise provider.
*   **SLA**: Guaranteed uptime and delivery performance.

## Usage Limits & Throttling
If you exceed your monthly email limit, sending will be paused until the next billing cycle or until you upgrade your plan. You can monitor your real-time usage in the **Settings > Billing** section of your dashboard.
